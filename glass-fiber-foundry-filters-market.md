<p><p>The Glass Fiber Foundry Filters market consists of two primary types: Low-Pressure Die Casting (LPDC) Machinery and Gravity Die Casting (GDC) Machinery. LPDC machinery utilizes low pressure to inject molten metal into molds, ensuring high precision and reduced porosity, making it ideal for complex parts. GDC machinery relies on gravity to fill molds, typically used for larger components and simpler shapes. Both technologies enhance the production quality in various industries, including automotive and aerospace, by improving the casting process.</p></p>
