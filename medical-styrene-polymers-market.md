Methacrylate-Butadiene-Styrene (MBS) provides flexibility and toughness. Styrene Butadiene Styrene (SBS) and Styrene-Ethylene-Butene-Styrene (SEBS) are known for flexibility and elastic properties, making them suitable for various medical applications.</p></p>
