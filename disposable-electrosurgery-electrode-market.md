<p><p>The disposable electrosurgery electrode market comprises primarily two control types: hand control and foot control. Hand control electrodes allow surgeons to operate directly with their hands, providing precise control during procedures. These are often designed for use in minimally invasive surgeries. On the other hand, foot control electrodes enable surgeons to maintain hand dexterity while activating the device using foot pedals. Both types are essential in modern surgical settings, enhancing efficiency and precision in electrosurgical techniques.</p></p>
