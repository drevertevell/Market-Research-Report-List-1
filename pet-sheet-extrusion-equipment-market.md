<p><strong>The PET Sheet Extrusion Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twin Screw Extruder</li><li>Single Screw Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET sheet extrusion equipment market features two primary types: twin screw extruders and single screw extruders. Twin screw extruders offer enhanced mixing and drainage, making them ideal for processing complex PET formulations and producing high-quality sheets with uniform thickness. In contrast, single screw extruders are simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for standard PET sheet production. The choice between the two depends on factors like production volume, material type, and desired product specifications, influencing efficiency and output.</p></p>
