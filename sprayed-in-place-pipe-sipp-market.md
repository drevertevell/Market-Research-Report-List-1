<p><p>Sprayed-In-Place Pipe (SIPP) is an innovative trenchless technology utilized for the rehabilitation and lining of existing pipelines. It involves applying a liquid polymer that hardens to form a structural liner, effectively enhancing the pipe's integrity while minimizing disruption to surrounding areas. SIPP is particularly advantageous for addressing leaks, corrosion, and structural deficiencies without the need for extensive excavation.</p><p>The SIPP market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing infrastructure investments and the urgent need for pipeline maintenance in aging urban areas.
