<p><p>The medical and pharmaceutical gases market is categorized into two main types: permanent gases and liquefiable gases. Permanent gases, such as oxygen and nitrogen, remain in gaseous form at room temperature and pressure, playing crucial roles in patient care and medical applications. Liquefiable gases, such as nitrous oxide and carbon dioxide, can be transformed into liquids under pressure and are often used for anesthesia and medical imaging. Both types are essential in healthcare for therapeutic and diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
