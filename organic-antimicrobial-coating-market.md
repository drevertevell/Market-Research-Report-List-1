<p><p>The organic antimicrobial coating market is divided into two main types based on molecular size: low molecular and macromolecule coatings. Low molecular antimicrobial coatings consist of small molecules that penetrate surfaces effectively, providing rapid antimicrobial action. In contrast, macromolecule coatings comprise larger polymers that create a protective barrier, offering prolonged antimicrobial protection and durability. Both types enhance surface hygiene and reduce microbial growth, making them essential in applications across healthcare, food processing, and consumer products.</p></p>
