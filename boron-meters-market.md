<p><p>The Boron Meter market primarily consists of two types: tank sensor boron meters and surface sensor boron meters. Tank sensor boron meters are designed for monitoring boron levels in liquid storage tanks, ensuring accurate measurements for industrial applications. In contrast, surface sensor boron meters monitor boron levels directly on soil or plant surfaces, providing crucial data for agricultural practices. Both types serve distinct sectors, catering to the needs of water treatment, agriculture, and chemical industries, enhancing efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
