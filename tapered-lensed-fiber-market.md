<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber</li><li>Multimode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tapered lensed fiber market is primarily divided into two types: single mode fiber and multimode fiber. Single mode fiber features a small core, allowing only one light mode to propagate, which results in high bandwidth and long-distance transmission with low loss. In contrast, multimode fiber has a larger core, enabling multiple light modes, making it suitable for shorter distances with higher data rates. Both types cater to various applications, including telecommunications, data centers, and industrial networking.</p></p>
