<p><p>The heat-resistant special graphite market encompasses various types, including isotropic graphite, extruded graphite, molded graphite, and others. Isotropic graphite exhibits uniform properties in all directions, making it suitable for high-performance applications. Extruded graphite, created through a continuous process, offers excellent density and electrical conductivity. Molded graphite, formed from compressed graphite powder, provides versatility and strength. Other types may include sheet graphite and flexible graphite, each catering to specific industrial needs, including aerospace, electronics, and energy sectors.</p></p>
