<p><p>The asphalt anti-stripping agent market is primarily categorized into two types: amine anti-stripping agents and amine-free anti-stripping agents. Amine anti-stripping agents utilize amine compounds to enhance the adhesion between asphalt and aggregates, improving durability and moisture resistance. In contrast, amine-free anti-stripping agents employ alternative chemistries to achieve similar outcomes without using amines, appealing to eco-conscious applications. Both types aim to enhance asphalt performance, particularly in regions with high rainfall or varying temperatures, ensuring longevity of road surfaces.</p></p>
