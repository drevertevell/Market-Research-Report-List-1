<p><p>Smoothing planes are categorized into three main market types: metal-bodied, wooden-bodied, and transitional. Metal-bodied planes are durable and often preferred for their stability and precision, featuring adjustable settings for fine-tuning. Wooden-bodied planes offer a traditional aesthetic and are favored by purists for their warm feel and vintage craftsmanship. Transitional planes combine elements of both, using a metal sole on a wooden body, blending modern durability with classic design. Each type serves specific preferences and user needs in woodworking.</p></p>
