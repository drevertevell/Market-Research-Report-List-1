<p><p>The Non-Invasive Blood Pressure Monitors market is primarily divided into digital and mechanical types. Digital monitors use electronic sensors to measure blood pressure automatically, providing quick and easy readings displayed on a screen, often with features like memory storage and irregular heartbeat detection. Mechanical monitors, such as sphygmomanometers, rely on a manual pump and an analog scale, requiring a healthcare provider for accurate readings. Each type has its own advantages, catering to different user needs in clinical and home settings.</p></p>
