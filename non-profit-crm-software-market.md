<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>Premise Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-profit CRM software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and premise-based systems. Cloud-based CRM solutions are hosted on the vendor's servers, offering flexibility, remote access, and lower upfront costs, making them ideal for organizations with limited IT resources. In contrast, premise-based CRM systems are installed on the organization’s own hardware, providing greater control over data and customization options but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to the unique needs of non-profits in managing donor relationships and engagement.</p></p>
