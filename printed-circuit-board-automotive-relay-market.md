<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) automotive relay market consists primarily of two types: Single Pole Single Throw (SPST) relays and Single Pole Double Throw (SPDT) relays. SPST relays control a single circuit, allowing simple on/off operation, making them ideal for basic switching applications in vehicles. In contrast, SPDT relays can switch between two different circuits, enabling more complex functionality and control options. Both types are essential in automotive systems for efficient power management and reliable operation of electrical components.</p></p>
