<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade dextrose glucose market consists of two primary types: API grade and excipient grade. API grade dextrose is utilized as an active pharmaceutical ingredient in formulations, serving essential roles in treatments, especially for energy replenishment. In contrast, excipient grade dextrose acts as a filler, stabilizer, or sweetener in various drug formulations, enhancing the delivery and efficacy of active ingredients. Both grades are crucial in pharmaceutical applications, meeting stringent purity and quality standards for safety and effectiveness.</p></p>
