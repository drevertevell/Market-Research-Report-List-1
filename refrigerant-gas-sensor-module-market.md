<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Refrigerant Gas Sensor Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) Sensors</li><li>Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigerant gas sensor module market primarily consists of two types: Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) sensors and Metal Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. NDIR sensors work by detecting the specific wavelength of infrared light absorbed by refrigerant gases, offering high sensitivity and selectivity. In contrast, MOS sensors utilize a change in electrical resistance in response to the presence of gases. NDIR sensors are favored for their accuracy in low concentrations, while MOS sensors are valued for their cost-effectiveness in various applications.</p></p>
