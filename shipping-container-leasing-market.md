<p><p>The shipping container leasing market primarily consists of two types: dry containers and reefer containers. Dry containers are standard steel boxes used for transporting non-perishable goods, offering durability and security. In contrast, reefer containers are equipped with refrigeration systems, specifically designed for perishable items like food and pharmaceuticals that require temperature control during transit. Both types are essential in global trade, facilitating the efficient movement of varied goods while catering to diverse shipping needs.</p></p>
