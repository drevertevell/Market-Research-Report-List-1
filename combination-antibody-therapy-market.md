Standard antibodies directly target specific antigens on cancer cells. Conjugated antibodies are engineered to deliver cytotoxic agents directly to tumor cells, minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissue. Bispecific antibodies simultaneously bind two different antigens, improving immune system engagement against tumors. Together, these therapies aim to improve patient outcomes by leveraging the strengths of various treatment modalities.</p></p>
