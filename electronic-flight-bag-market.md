<p><strong>The Electronic Flight Bag Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Portable EFB</li><li>Installed EFB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Flight Bag (EFB) market is categorized into two main types: Portable EFBs and Installed EFBs. Portable EFBs are lightweight, tablet-like devices used by pilots to access essential flight information and applications on the go, allowing flexibility and ease of use. Installed EFBs, on the other hand, are integrated into the aircraft's cockpit systems, offering comprehensive data and functionalities that enhance operational efficiency and safety but require more complex installation and maintenance. Both types improve flight operations significantly.</p></p>
