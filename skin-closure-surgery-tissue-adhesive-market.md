<p><p>The skin closure surgery tissue adhesive market primarily comprises two types: polyurethane-based and cyanoacrylate-based adhesives. Polyurethane-based adhesives offer flexibility and continuous moisture resistance, making them suitable for dynamic wound environments. In contrast, cyanoacrylate-based adhesives provide rapid bonding and effective barrier properties, ideal for quick closure in surgical settings. While both types serve to promote healing and minimize scarring, their distinct properties cater to varying clinical needs and preferences in wound management practices.</p></p>
