<p><p>Venturi scrubbers are pollution control devices used to remove particulate matter and gaseous pollutants from industrial exhaust streams. They operate based on the Venturi effect, where gas is forced through a throat in the scrubber, creating a drop in pressure that facilitates the contact between the gas and the scrubbing liquid. This efficient design allows for the effective capture of a wide range of contaminants, making Venturi scrubbers suitable for various industries, including power generation, chemical processing, and manufacturing.</p><p>The Venturi Scrubbers Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing regulatory pressures related to air quality and emissions control.
