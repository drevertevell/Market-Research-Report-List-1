<p><p>Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT) involves administering oxygen at elevated pressures to enhance healing and treat various medical conditions. The market for HBOT devices is categorized into two main types: monoplace and multiplace systems. Monoplace HBOT devices accommodate a single patient in a chamber, ensuring a controlled environment for individual treatment. In contrast, multiplace HBOT devices are larger chambers that can treat multiple patients simultaneously, making them more suitable for facilities that handle higher patient volumes. Both types play crucial roles in therapeutic applications.</p></p>
