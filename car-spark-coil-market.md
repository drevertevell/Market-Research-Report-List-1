<p><ul><li>Single-Spark</li><li>Multi-Spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car spark coil market is segmented into single-spark and multi-spark types. Single-spark coils provide a single electrical discharge per engine cycle, typically used in standard ignition systems. In contrast, multi-spark coils can produce multiple sparks, enhancing combustion efficiency and improving performance in high-performance and racing vehicles. The choice between these types depends on the vehicle's design, performance requirements, and desired fuel efficiency, with multi-spark coils often preferred for their ability to optimize engine output.</p></p>
