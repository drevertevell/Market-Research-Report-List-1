<p><strong>The DR Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect DR Detector</li><li>Direct DR Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital radiography (DR) detector market is primarily divided into two types: direct and indirect DR detectors. Direct DR detectors convert X-rays into digital signals using semiconductor materials, offering higher image quality and faster processing. Indirect DR detectors, on the other hand, first convert X-rays into light, which is then transformed into a digital signal through photodetectors. These systems are generally more affordable and have a wider adoption in various healthcare settings, balancing cost and performance effectively.</p></p>
