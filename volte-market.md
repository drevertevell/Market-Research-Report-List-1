<p><p>VoLTE (Voice over LTE) market can be segmented into several types: </p><p>1. **VoIMS (Voice over IMS)**: This involves delivering voice services through the IP Multimedia Subsystem, enabling rich communication features over LTE networks.</p><p>2. **CSFB (Circuit Switched Fall Back)**: This method allows calls to be routed through circuit-switched networks when LTE coverage is unavailable, ensuring service continuity.</p><p>3. **Others**: This category encompasses various hybrid solutions and emerging technologies that facilitate voice communication across different network types, enhancing user experience and network efficiency.</p></p>
